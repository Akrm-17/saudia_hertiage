The Magic of the Red Sea

The Red Sea is one of the most important marine ecosystems on Earth. Its waters are exceptionally warm and clear, providing an ideal environment for coral reefs and diverse marine species. Jeddah's unique location on its shores has made it a historical gateway and a modern tourist destination.
